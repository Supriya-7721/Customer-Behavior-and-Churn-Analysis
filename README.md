# Customer 360 Analytics Project
## Overview
Developed an end-to-end customer analytics solution to analyze customer behaviour, revenue performance, acquisition channels, engagement patterns, and churn drivers using Python, SQL, and Power BI.
---
## Business Objective 
- Analyse customer spending behaviour
- Identify churn patterns
- Evaluate acquisition channel performance
- Understand membership tier contribution
- Generate actionable business recommendations
---
## Dataset
**Records:** 8,000 Customers
## Features
- Customer Demographics
- Membership Tier
- Purchase History
- Revenue Metrics
- Acquisition Channels
- Device Preferences
- Customer Engagement
- Churn Status

## Key Insights
- Generated **$12.47M revenue from **132K orders** across **8000 customers**.
- **United States** contributed the highest revenue among all countries.
- **Organic Search** was the highest revenue-generating acquisition channel.
- More than **54% of customers preferred mobile devices**, highligting strong mobile engagement.
 **Free-tier customers accounted for the largest share of churn**, indicating membership conversion opportunities.
- Customer aged **25-45 years** represented the dominanat customer segment.
- Newsletter subscribers showed slightly better retention compared to non-subscribers.
- Global markets are currently under-penetrated. France and Germany show combined revenue gaps over $10M.
- High acquisition volume, but 'Free' users exhibit the highest churn susceptibility.
- Strong organic brand equity, but a critical 20% conversion gap exists on mobile.

---
## Business Recommendations
- Increase Free-to-Paid membership conversions through loyalty programs.
- Prioritize SEO investments due to strong Organic Search performance.
- Continue optimizing mobile user experience and campaigns.
- Launch retentions campaigns targetting inactive customers.
- Reduce product return rates through quality and fulfillments improvements.
